Once upon a time, in the land of Pixelia, there lived a brave [color=blue]knight[/color] named Sir Cedric. 
He was tasked with defeating a fearsome dragon that had been terrorizing the kingdom.
The [color=red]dragon[/color], known as Dracus, was a master of the Shannon Switching game and had never been defeated. 
With his wits and courage, Sir Cedric challenged Dracus to a game of Shannon Switching.
